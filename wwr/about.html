History

White water rafting is a thrilling and challenging sport that has been around for centuries. It was first developed in the 19th century by European explorers who were looking for a way to navigate the treacherous rivers of the American West. Today, white water rafting is a popular recreational activity that is enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.
